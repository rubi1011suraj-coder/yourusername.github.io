🕳️ What is a Black Hole?

A black hole is a place in space where gravity is so strong that nothing can escape from it—not even light.

Interesting Fact:
Scientists captured the first image of a black hole in 2019.

⭐ How are Black Holes Formed?

When a very massive star runs out of fuel, it collapses under its own gravity. If the remaining core is massive enough, it becomes a black hole.

Did You Know?
Our Milky Way galaxy has a supermassive black hole called Sagittarius A*.

🚀 Conclusion

Black holes are among the most fascinating objects in the universe. Scientists continue studying them to learn more about space and time.
